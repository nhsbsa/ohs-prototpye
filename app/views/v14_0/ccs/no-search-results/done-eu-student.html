What happens next

To be entitled to an EHIC, a UK student studying in the EU, Norway, Iceland, Lichtenstein or Switzerland must have started their studies abroad before 31 December 2020. In addition, their study period must continue after 31 December 2020.

What evidence must the customer submit?

In all cases, the customer must submit the following evidence:

A letter from their educational institution confirming all of the following:

  • the date their study period in the EU, Norway, Iceland, Liechtenstein or Switzerland started or is due to start (this must be before 31 December 2020).
  • the date their study period in that same country will end (this must be after 31 December 2020).
  • the name and address of their educational institution.
  • details of the qualification they are studying for.

The customer has automatically been sent an email with the above information and instructions on how to submit their evidence.

Evidence for Third Country Nationals

If the customer is not a UK or EU national, they must also submit evidence of their visa or alternative evidence to confirm they have paid the Immigration Health Surcharge. If they have not paid this charge, they will not be entitled to an EHIC.

Where should evidence be sent?

Evidence should be sent to nhsbsa.ehicapplicationsohs@nhs.net

The customer will have received an email with this address in. The customer should ensure that, when sending evidence to support their application, they include the following details:

  • their EHIC reference number (this will be within the email they receive)
  • their full name
  • their date of birth
